Market breadth measures how many stocks rose — not how far the headline index moved. When more than half the constituents advance, the market's strength is broad. When the figure sinks below fifty, a narrowing handful of heavyweights is carrying the tape while the rank and file quietly retreat.

The gauge above counts the share of the entire IDX listing — roughly 950 stocks — that closed higher today. Below, the panels trace the index itself, its breadth, and its momentum back through the years.
